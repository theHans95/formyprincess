You're My Sederhana Sempurna 🤍

Dear Lovi 🦋

Sometimes I terfikir, maybe Allah memang tak menghantar seseorang yang sempurna untuk I. Instead, He sent me you.

Seorang wanita bernama Lovi.

Seorang yang ada hari baik dan hari yang berat. Seorang yang overthink, mudah penat, kadang-kadang hilang semangat, tapi masih berusaha bertahan walaupun tak ramai yang nampak.

Seorang yang selalu memberi lebih daripada apa yang orang sedar, and selalu memikul lebih banyak daripada apa yang orang sangka.

And somehow, despite everything that life has thrown at you, you're still here. Still trying, still loving, and still choosing to wake up and face another day.

And honestly, sayang...

I think that's one of the most beautiful things about you. 🤍

And somehow, dalam setiap sisi diri sayang yang sederhana itu, I found a feeling that I never found in anyone else.

You are not perfect, sayang.

But to me, you are perfectly enough. 🤍

I know I mungkin takkan pernah dapat faham 100% segala yang sayang rasa dan lalui.

But that's okay.

Because loving you was never about understanding everything perfectly. It's about choosing to stay, choosing to learn, and choosing to understand you a little more every day.

I masih belajar tentang diri sayang. Belajar perkara yang buat sayang tersenyum, perkara yang buat sayang penat, and perkara yang buat hati sayang tenang.

And honestly, I don't mind spending my whole life learning about you.

Because I never fell in love with a perfect version of you.

I fell in love with a woman named Lovi, with her smile, her tears, her overthinking, and every little part that makes her, her.

And until today, loving you is still one of the most beautiful things that ever happened to me.

Sayang bukan sekadar seseorang yang I cinta.

Sayang adalah salah satu anugerah terindah yang pernah Allah hadirkan dalam hidup I. 🤍🦋

And sayang, there's something I hope you know too.

I know I bukan seorang yang sempurna. I know I ada banyak kekurangan, banyak kelemahan, and there are still so many things that I need to improve.

Sometimes I make mistakes, sometimes I overthink too much, and sometimes I wonder whether I'm loving you in the right way.

But one thing I know for sure...

I may not be perfect,

But my love and effort for you will always be sincere. 🤍

I mungkin tak boleh menjanjikan hubungan yang sempurna.

But I can promise that I will never stop learning, never stop trying, and never stop putting effort into the woman that I love.

Because if sayang is willing to choose me despite all my flaws, then I want to spend my days becoming someone better, not only for myself, but also for us.

And honestly, there's one prayer that I always keep quietly in my heart.

Ya Allah, thank You for letting me meet the woman that I love.

And if it is Your will, please continue giving me the chance and opportunity to make her happy.

Semoga Allah sentiasa memberi I peluang dan kesempatan untuk terus membahagiakan wanita yang I cinta ni.

Berilah I kekuatan untuk terus memilih sayang, kesabaran untuk terus memahami sayang, and jangan pernah biarkan hati I penat untuk terus mengusahakan sayang.

Because among billions of people in this world, Allah allowed me to meet someone who became my comfort, my peace, and one of the most beautiful blessings in my life.

I don't know what the future holds. But as long as Allah still gives me the chance, I want to continue loving you properly.

Not only on your happy days, but also on the days when sayang rasa penat, kosong, lost, and even on the days when sayang rasa diri sayang terlalu rumit untuk dicintai.

Because you never have to become someone else for me to stay.

You never have to hide the difficult parts of yourself.

You never have to be perfect.

You just have to be you.

Maybe to other people, sayang nampak biasa.

But to me, sayang will always be someone special.

Someone who taught me that happiness doesn't always come in perfect forms.

Sometimes happiness is simply hearing your voice, seeing your smile, and knowing that you're still here. 🤍

And if one day life becomes heavy, or sayang starts doubting yourself again, I hope this little letter will remind sayang of one thing.

You have always been worthy of love.

And among billions of people in this world, I'm grateful that Allah let me love you. 🤍

So if one day sayang asks me why I love you so much, the answer is simple.

Because you never had to be perfect for me to love you.

You just had to be you.

And that's why, out of everyone in this world...

You will always be my favourite person. 👑

My beautiful princess. 🤍

My anugerah terindah. 🦋

And my sederhana sempurna. ❤️

With all the love in my heart 🤍

— Hans 🐯
Your biggest supporter.
Your safe place.
And someone who will continue choosing you, every single day. 🦋
